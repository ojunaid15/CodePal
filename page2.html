About

CodePal aims to bring girls from different regions of the world in middle schools and high schools and foster their interest in the world of technology and coding.

We want to use STEM education to inspite and influence these females to explore the limitless world of STEM.

Community

Our goal is not only to create a network of coder girls, but also a support system. We hope to help girls in developing nations grow their interest in STEM.

What will you find here? A support system. Resources. Most Importantly, a group of girls eager to help each other.
