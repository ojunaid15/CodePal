CodePal

Where Girl Coders Everywhere Unite

About

CodePal aims to bring girls from different locations around the world in middle schools and high schools to foster their interest in the world of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.

We want to use Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, or more commonly known as, STEM education to inspite and influence these young females to explore the limitless world of what STEM can offer them.

They will build life friendships and partnerships to help each other grown and work on future projects, or even startups!

Our Goal

Our goal is to have more girls in the tedch world by the year 2040. Brining girls from different regions around the world together, they will not only create a network of coder girls, but also a support system. We hope to help girls in developing nations grow their interest in STEM.

What Will You Find Here?

Resources. A support system. Most Importantly, a group of girls eager to help each other.
